About Us


We are the Brooklyn Daily Minyan project, an traditional egalitarian daily minyan meeting in the CBE chapel and davining in Nusach Ashkenaz. The minyan was started and concieved by Rachel Geballe and Liora Cobin in the summer of 2023 as a minyan for those looking for daily trad-egal prayer and a welcoming and consistent place to say Kaddish. Originally meeting only once a week, we now meets five times per week at varying times. After the minyan, we frequently schmooz with amazing baked goods and tea brought by members of the community.
